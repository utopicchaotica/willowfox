Hearth and Home

Your curious nature has led you to cross the threshold from the mundane to the fantastic, historic, and futuristic! Welcome to our hearth and home! We are a consortium of word and image bards come together to spellbind you with our magical tales both individually and collaboratively. More about the Willow and the Fox

The first seed of our book forest has just sprouted. We’ve named it, The Monocle of Shai. It will take time for the other seeds to break through the earth, so please be patient as they grow.

Fox’s Den is our children’s book project where our authors and artists work together to produce diverse, whimsical, and imaginative children’s stories at an affordable price.

Willow’s Hollow is our middle grade book project where our authors help adolescents navigate the maze between childhood and adulthood.

Skulk’s Grove (coming Spring 2021) is our young adult book project where our authors challenge young adults to stretch their wings and fly.
